About me

I am James Bain, I was born in Western Australia and grew up in the Wheatbelt in a small town near Toodyay. I moved to Perth to attend secondary school at Guildford Grammar School. It was there that I developed a love for the sciences including human biology, chemistry, and physics. Since leaving school I have pursued many careers, none of which I wholeheartedly grew attached to. I have worked in the mines for a couple of years, worked in the concreting business for a year, washed windows for a semester, and picked produce for Coles over a summer. In these professions, I would often work as part of a small team but would frequently take the initiative to organize the working environment suitably for myself and my team members. I am also comfortable working independently and pride myself in always being extremely safety conscious. However, I now have majorly shifted career paths and settled on support work. I have thoroughly enjoyed my time working in this area and it has given me a great appreciation for helping others.
